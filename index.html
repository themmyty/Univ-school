Our Success

Our US applicants have received offers from Harvard, Stanford, Princeton, Yale, Columbia, MIT, UPenn, Brown, Cornell, Dartmouth, Caltech, UC Berkeley, UCLA, UChicago, Duke, Northwestern and all schools in the US Top 50, while our UK applicants have been accepted to Oxford, Cambridge, UCL, Imperial, LSE, King’s College London, the University of Edinburgh and many other top UK institutions. More specifically, many of our students have 2, 3, 4, 5 or sometimes 10 or more offers to choose from. In these cases their strategist will work with them on their ‘wonderful dilemma’, helping them choose for example between their Ivy League acceptances to identify the best fit them.
